      Review and Sign

      In this section:

      If you're a Sertifi Admin+ role, and you're using Sertifi eSignatures, you can countersign documents sent to your clients.

      To countersign a document:

      1. Log in to your Sertifi portal.

      2. Click Sign from the home screen, or click the Sign tab. The Sign screen opens.

      3. Click Signing to view a list of files with documents awaiting countersignature.

      4. Click the name of the file you want to open to countersign. The signing room opens.

      5. Click Review and Sign. The document opens.

      6. Click Get Started at the top of the window.

      7. Enter your countersignature in the required locations.

      8. Click Finish & Submit Electronically once all required fields are filled out.
